To learn how to implement these feeds, see the [Ethereum Examples for Consuming Data Feeds](https://docs.chain.link/data-feeds/using-data-feeds).

Note, onchain equity and ETF assets are only traded during standard market hours (9:30 am - 4pm ET M-F). Using these feeds outside of those windows is not recommended.

> It is recommended to use the [L2 Sequencer Health Flag](https://docs.chain.link/chainlink-sequencer-health-flag) for all contracts on the Arbitrum network.
